D3Times: How does age, household income or one's poverty level affect health factors like obesity, smoking, and lack of healthcare? Use the interactive chart below to explore data from the United States Census Bureau. Hover over each state for details. Click a different axis label to gain additional insights on each factor.

Analysis: In general there appear to be a slight increase in the health factors of obesity, smoking and lack of healthcare among states with higher rates of poverty.
There appears to be a general trend between household income and the same health factors with higher rates of obesity and smoking among those in households with a median income below $40k.
The trend between age and all three health factors is less pronounced.
Data Source: 2014 U.S. Census Data Survey
